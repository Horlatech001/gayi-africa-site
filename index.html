About US

Green Africa Youths Initiative started in June 2018 with four people including the founder, Ibrahim Nurudeen (ACA), Ajibola Habib (M.Sc), Sulyman Kunmbo Salamat(M.Sc) and Ibrahim Abdulqadir(M.Sc). It was initially named Green Global Life Initiative. It was later renamed to Green Africa Youths Initiative. Green Africa Youths Initiative was incorporated with corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in the year 2019. These 4 people started the journey of GAYI with the burning aspiration and passion to serve Africa youths.

Back to School Project

In line with our core focus of sustainable future for young generation. We donated school material to 20 vulnerable students in Kwara state.

Beneficiaries are

☆ Banni Community Secondary School, Ilorin

☆ Government Secondary School Baboko, Ilorin

Beneficiary Country and State: Kwara State, Nigeria.

Year of Project: 2019

Source of fund: Solicitations and Fund from Volunteer Members

GREEN AFRICA YOUTH CAMPAIGN

This is a campaign targeted at the young students in secondary school. The aim of this project is to inspire and educate on a better life through their career, entrepreneurship pursuit. Individual who have excel in their various field was invited as guest speaker.

Beneficiaries are

☆ Future Leader Model Secondary School, Ilorin

☆ Federal Government College, Ilorin

☆ Kwara State School for Special needs

☆ Queens Elizabeth Secondary School, Ilorin Kwara State

☆ Okelele Secondary School Ilorin, Kwara State

☆ Government Secondary School Amule, Ilorin

Beneficiary Country and State: Kwara State, Nigeria.

Year of Project: 2019

Source of fund: Fund from Volunteer and Members
