About US

Green Africa Youths Initiative started in June 2018 with four people including the founder, Ibrahim Nurudeen (ACA), Ajibola Habib (M.Sc), Sulyman Kunmbo Salamat(M.Sc) and Ibrahim Abdulqadir(M.Sc). It was initially named Green Global Life Initiative. It was later renamed to Green Africa Youths Initiative. Green Africa Youths Initiative was incorporated with corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in the year 2019. These 4 people started the journey of GAYI with the burning aspiration and passion to serve Africa youths.

We believe Africa can rise. We have a deep conviction that we can build a better Africa where youths thrive, green and sustainable environment can be sustained and sustainable future for young and future generation. This philosophy shall guide all our actions. We are ready to work with individuals, corporations, counterpart NGOs, Agencies who share this same ideal and philosophy to achieve our goals.

OUR MISSION

To unleash Africa super power by promoting entrepreneurship, climate action and sustainable living.

OUR VISION

To be a champion and number one Africa Indigenous Initiative driving the sustainable development of Africa.
